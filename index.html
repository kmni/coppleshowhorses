Quality Training for Champions

Our goal is to produce nationally competitive horses for the open division as well as for his amateurs. We offer a full service training facility located in the Nisqually Valley, forty five minutes from the Seattle International Airport, with the best care and attention to detail. Whether it's a futurity prospect, amateur or open horse, we have what any Arabian horse enthusiast is looking for. Give us a call today to see the barn and our beautiful show horses!

We will create a customized training program to suit you and your horse, with lasting results. Whether it be purchasing a new horse, marketing, lessons or showing, we look forward to the opportunity of assisting you with your needs. Visitors are always welcome!

Ron Copple

Ron has been training and showing Arabian and Half Arabian horses since 1996, with numerous Regional and National wins in the Western and Hunter Pleasure divisions. His background with horses started with his family as a child and the horses continue to be the driving force behind his love for the profession. Over the years he has learned from several nationally known trainers, Larry Lewis Jr., and Brett & Marjie Becker. In 2006 his hard work was rewarded with a nomination by his peers for the APAHA "Rising Star" award.

Yvonne Copple

An accomplished and talented trainer with experience gained through her acquaintance with some of the industry's finest, such as Bob Battaglia, Bret Becker, Natalie Jones, just to name a few. Hard work and dedication has propelled her into the limelight with multiple National Championships, Top Tens as well as Regional and Scottsdale roses in the Hunter Pleasure, Country Pleasure, Show Hack, Sidesaddle, Saddle seat, Hunt Seat Equitation, Western Pleasure and Native Costume, Sport Horse and Dressage.
